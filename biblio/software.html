sfront   An optimizing compiler for the MPEG 4 Structured Audio language, sfront converts SA files into efficient C programs that generate audio. The programs sfront creates may be used interactively as a musical instrument or audio effects processor. Sfront supports network musical performance and acts as the reference implementation for RTP MIDI.
