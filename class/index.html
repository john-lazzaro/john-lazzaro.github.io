EECS 152

CS 194-6

CS 250

In Fall 2009-2013, I was a part of teams that taught CS 250 (VLSI Design). See the 2012 class home page for a typical semester. Helping out with CS 250 was a deja-vu experience for me, as it was the first class I taught at Cal (Spring 1994).

Music 209

I co-taught Music 209 (Advanced Topics in Computer Music) in Spring 2006, with Music Professor David Wessel (deceased). Follow date link for class website.
